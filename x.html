Banana bread recipe video | How to make banana bread recipe

By swasthi , on July 20, 2019 , 47 Comments , Jump to Recipe

Banana bread recipe video – Simple quick banana bread recipe with video and step by step photos. I don’t bake often but whenever we have overripe bananas I bake banana bread, banana muffins, banana cake and moist chocolate banana cake . This banana bread recipe is one of our favorites and has been tried by me so many times and has always turned out great. If you are a beginner do check out the video I have shared below.

This banana bread recipe uses a single egg so the results are too good and is lighter than a eggless banana bread. This is a no fail recipe, even a beginner can bake this with wonderful results very easily.

You can also check this delicious eggless banana bread that’s vegan too and this eggless banana muffins recipe .

Flour:
This works good with whole wheat flour (atta) or plain flour (maida) and even with half of each. I have used whole wheat pastry flour which yields the same results as using half regular whole wheat flour (atta) and half plain flour. Earlier I have also made this using only regular whole wheat flour, the difference is a slightly denser banana bread.

I have used organic turbinado sugar, any kind of sugar just works good here. The color of the cake may be lighter if made using white sugar. Since I used the turbinado sugar the banana bread turned out to be lightly golden to brown.

Banana bread

Banana bread – Light, moist and delicious quick bread that can be served as a snack or breakfast.
Pin Recipe Print Recipe

For best results follow the step-by-step photos above the recipe card

Prep Time 10 minutes
Cook Time 1 hour
Total Time 1 hour 10 minutes
Servings 8
Author Swasthi

Ingredients (US cup = 240ml )

  • 1 ½ cups flour or 180 grams , (plain flour, whole wheat flour or whole wheat pastry flour)
  • 2 bananas , ripe (medium, about 6 inches long)
  • 75 to 80 grams butter or 1/3 cup melted
  • ¾ cup sugar (organic)
  • 1 egg ,large , beaten
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 Pinch salt
  • ½ tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
Advertisement

Instructions

Preparation

  • Sieve together flour, cinnamon, salt and baking soda. Set this aside.
  • Grease a 8 by 4 inch loaf pan with butter.
  • Beat the egg and set aside.
  • Preheat the oven to 160 C for atleast 15 mins.
  • Mash the bananas very well either with a masher or puree them.
  • Add sugar and vanilla. Mix well until sugar dissolves.
  • Add butter and beaten egg.
  • Mix well.
  • Mix together flour in 2 parts with the wet ingredients just until combined. Avoid over mixing and make sure there are no lumps.

Making banana bread

  • Transfer the batter to the greased pan.
  • Bake for 60 minutes or until a skewer inserted comes out clean. You may need to adjust the baking time by 5 to 10 mins depending on your oven. But keep an eye after 55 mins. Mine was done exactly after 60 mins.
  • Transfer the banana bread to a rack.
  • Cool completely and cut with a bread knife.
  • Banana bread can be served with milk or tea.
